Alex Pinch


I'm an aspiring biologist and programmer. I grew up in Grey County, Ontario, and have recently completed a BSc. in Biology from the University of Victoria.

Previously, I've worked with the Gorge Waterway Action Society to assess the diversity of local bees in Esquimalt Gorge Park, British Columbia. Before that, I worked at the Owens Lab at UVic, where I did some population genetics modelling and performed DNA quality checks on kelp. I've also worked on some projects with the University of Victoria Horticulture Club, including the newsletter and a local invasive species list.

I spend my free time learning Italian, reading Dune, and staring at nature wondering about its insides.
